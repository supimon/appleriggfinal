Our Board

Alex Scott

- Executive Chairman, Applerigg, Sandaire and Lord North Street

- Non-Executive Chairman, Sandaire (Singapore)

- Non-Executive Director, Yealand Group

Alex is Chairman of Applerigg and a number of its subsidiary companies

Having overseen the sale of his family's Provincial Group businesses in 1994, Aex founded Sandaire in 1996 as a multi-family office to provide advice and investement services to his own family and to other families and foundations in similar situations.

He is a non-executive director of his family's investment holding company, and of several private companies. He is a trustee of the Grosvenor Estate, Chairman of Wheatsheaf Investments and of the Grosvenor pension plan.

A leading participant in the world of family businesses, Alex co-founded and is the Life-President of the Institute of Family Businesses (UK). He has several philanthrophical responsibilities and commitments.

Alex read Politics, Philosophy and Economics at Exeter College, Oxford and holds an MBA from IMD, Lausanne.
